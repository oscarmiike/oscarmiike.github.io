\href{https://travis-ci.org/floatdrop/pinkie}{\texttt{ }} \href{https://coveralls.io/github/floatdrop/pinkie?branch=master}{\texttt{ }}

There are \href{https://github.com/promises-aplus/promises-spec/blob/master/implementations.md\#standalone}{\texttt{ tons of Promise implementations}} out there, but all of them focus on browser compatibility and are often bloated with functionality.
