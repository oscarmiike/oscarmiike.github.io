Parse, convert, fingerprint and use SSH keys (both public and private) in pure node -- no {\ttfamily ssh-\/keygen} or other external dependencies.

Supports RSA, DSA, ECDSA (nistp-\/\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}) and ED25519 key types, in PEM (PKCS\#1, PKCS\#8) and Open\+SSH formats.
