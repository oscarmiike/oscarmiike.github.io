It\textquotesingle{}s recommended to open an issue before sending a pull request to avoid unnecessary work. There are quite few areas we consider to be out of scope for this library. Idea is to add few generic string helpers for Javascript. For example anything related to internationalization or is too language specific is out of scope.\hypertarget{md__c_1_2_users_2_s_t_r_i_d_e_r_2source_2repos_2_internal_a_p_i_2_internal_a_p_i_2wwwroot_2lib_2a1ac0260594a2aa2189ba7f85db43a79_autotoc_md11982}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Release checklist}{Release checklist}}\label{md__c_1_2_users_2_s_t_r_i_d_e_r_2source_2repos_2_internal_a_p_i_2_internal_a_p_i_2wwwroot_2lib_2a1ac0260594a2aa2189ba7f85db43a79_autotoc_md11982}
