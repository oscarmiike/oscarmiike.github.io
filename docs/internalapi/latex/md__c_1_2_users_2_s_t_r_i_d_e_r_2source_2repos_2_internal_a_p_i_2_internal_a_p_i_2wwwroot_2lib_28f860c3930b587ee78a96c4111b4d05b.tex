Converts time between units.

Available units\+: {\ttfamily millisecond}, {\ttfamily second}, {\ttfamily minute}, {\ttfamily hour}, {\ttfamily day}, {\ttfamily week}. Abbreviations\+: {\ttfamily ms}, {\ttfamily s}, {\ttfamily m}, {\ttfamily h}, {\ttfamily d}, {\ttfamily w}.

We do {\bfseries{not}} support year and month as a time unit since their values are not fixed.

The default {\ttfamily destination\+Unit} is {\ttfamily ms}.
