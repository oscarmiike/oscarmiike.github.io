 The Reactive Extensions for Java\+Script Lite version is a lightweight version of the Reactive Extensions for Java\+Script which covers most of the day to day operators you might use all in a single library. Functionality such as bridging to events, promises, callbacks, Node.\+js-\/style callbacks, time-\/based operations and more are built right in.
